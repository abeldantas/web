Abel Dantas

I'm a software engineer focused on distributed systems and gamification.

Over the past decade, I've founded multiple organizations and led technical teams. I was founding president of CTVC, Portugal's first tech cooperative, an organization I remain part of today. I served as CTO of a blockchain gaming company for six years. Currently, I'm exploring how distributed ledgers and gamification can create verifiable ownership systems and coordination across organizations—with fashion retail as the application domain.

I'm pursuing a PhD at University of Porto, where I also teach. My research is on Distributed BFT Ledgers with CRDTs and my coordinator is Professor Carlos Baquero. I have recently published some of my work at PaPoC 2025 (ACM). I also happen to teach in Viana do Castelo at IPVC.

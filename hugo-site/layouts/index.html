My Website

Abel Dantas

I'm an engineer and researcher focused on systems that work without centralized coordination and the mechanisms that drive engagement within them.
Photograph of me taken by my good friend João Peixoto, circa 2024.

I'm a PhD candidate at University of Porto, working with Professor Carlos Baquero on Byzantine fault tolerance and distributed ledger systems. My research on CRDTs in peer-to-peer VR was published at PaPoC 2025 (ACM). I serve as Guest Professor at both IPVC and FEUP.

My research interests span distributed ledgers, Byzantine fault tolerance, and gamification systems. I founded CTVC, northern Portugal's first tech cooperative—a decentralized structure for tech professionals. Over the past decade, I've led technical teams in gaming, blockchain infrastructure, and applied research.

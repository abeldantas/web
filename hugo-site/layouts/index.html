Abel Dantas

I'm a founder and engineer focused on building systems that work without centralized coordination.

Over the past decade, I've founded multiple organizations and led technical teams. I served as CTO of a blockchain gaming company for six years. Currently, I'm exploring how distributed ledgers and gamification can create verifiable ownership systems and coordination across organizations—with fashion retail as the application domain.

I'm pursuing a PhD at University of Porto with Professor Carlos Baquero on Byzantine fault tolerance and distributed ledgers, with research interests in gamification. My work on CRDTs in peer-to-peer VR was published at PaPoC 2025 (ACM). I also teach as Guest Professor at IPVC and FEUP.

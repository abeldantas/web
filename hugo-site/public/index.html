Abel Dantas

I am a founder, researcher and software engineer.
Photograph of me taken by my good friend João Peixoto, circa 2024. My research focus and interests are in the areas of Distributed Systems, Byzantine Fault Tolerance, Decentralized Resource Networks, Gamification, AI and ML.

I'm a PhD candidate at University of Porto, collaborating with Professor Carlos Baquero. My research focuses on distributed systems, exploring consensus-free Byzantine fault tolerance and its applications in Web3, digital assets, and marketplaces.

I am also a Guest Professor at both IPVC and FEUP. I have extensive experience in the gaming and blockchain industries, having led several technical teams over the last decade.
